Mission Statement

We create timeless pieces of art for independent women that are proud to show their femininity.

Our pieces are made of sustainably sourced materials and excess fabric.

Our mission is to make sure that middle-eastern women can feel beautiful while being ethical.

We aim to engage both governments and society in working towards a sustainable present for future generations.
